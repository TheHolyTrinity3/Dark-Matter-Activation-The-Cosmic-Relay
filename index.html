Inert Dark Matter

Dark matter floats everywhere in the universe — invisible, passing through all matter without interaction. Inert and undetected.

Zone 02
The Heliosphere

The boundary where the Sun's solar wind meets interstellar space. A magnetic bubble 100 AU across enclosing the entire solar system.

Zone 03
Activated Matter

Hypothesis: dark matter crossing the heliosphere boundary is activated by UV radiation and solar wind, changing state and carrying evolutionary data toward life.

Hypothesis — Untested

Dark matter particles vibrate upon entering the solar field. UV radiation activates those vibrations. Solar wind spreads activated particles through the solar system, where they interact with biological DNA — delivering minute evolutionary updates, generation by generation.
